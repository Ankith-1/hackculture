Top 10 places to Visit
Let the experts guide you to the best of this mesmerizing destination

  • Jagannath Temple

    Jagannath Temple is a Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Jagannath in Puri, Odisha, India. It's one of the char dham pilgrimage sites and a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

    Learn More

  • Konark Temple

    Konark Sun Temple is a 13th-century Hindu temple in Konark, Odisha, India. It's dedicated to the sun god Surya and famous for its ornate carvings and architectural style.

    Learn More

  • Puri Sea Beach

    Puri Beach is a popular tourist destination located in Puri, Odisha, India. It's known for its long stretch of golden sand, serene atmosphere, and scenic views of the Bay of Bengal.

    Learn More

  • Chandravaga Sea Beach

    Chandrabhaga Beach is located near Konark, Odisha, India. It is famous for its black sand and clear water. It is named after the Chandra Bhaga River.

    Learn More

  • Puri Konark Marine Drive

    The Puri Konark Marine Drive is a scenic coastal road in Odisha, India. It connects Puri and Konark and offers stunning views of forest on one side and the Bay of Bengal on the other.

    Learn More

  • Raghurajpur Art & craft Village

    Raghurajpur is a heritage village in Odisha, India. It is famous for its Pattachitra paintings, palm leaf engravings, and traditional crafts. The village is home to over 200 traditional craftsmen and artists.

    Learn More

  • Lokanath Temple

    Lokanath Temple is a Hindu temple located in Puri, Odisha, India. It is one of the most revered shrines dedicated to Lord Shiva. It is believed to be the oldest temples in Puri built in the 12th century.

    Learn More

  • Mausi Ma Temple

    Mausi Ma Temple is a Hindu temple located in Puri, Odisha, India. It is dedicated to the goddess Maa Manikeswari. It is one of the oldest and most revered shrines in Puri. The temple is considered to be over 1000 years old.

    Learn More

  • Sakhigopal Temple

    Sakhigopal Temple is a Hindu temple located in the town of Sakhigopal, Odisha, India. It is dedicated to Lord Krishna and is considered one of the sacred shrines in the state. The temple is believed to be over 500 years old..

    Learn More

  • Satapada(Chilika Lake)

    Satapada is a tourist destination part of chilika lake where it meets the sea. It is a picturesque fishing village situated at the mouth of Chilika Lake. It is famous for its dolphins and bird watching opportunities.

    Learn More
